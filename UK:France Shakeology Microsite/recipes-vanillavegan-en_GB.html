Carrot Cake Energy Balls
  • 1½ scoops Vanilla Plant-Based Vegan Shakeology
  • 65 g / ½ cup dry rolled oats
  • 80 g / ⅓ cup all-natural smooth almond butter
  • 4 pitted dates, coarsely chopped
  • 20 g / ¼ cup unsalted chopped walnuts
  • 40 g / ¾ cup shredded carrots (approx. 2½ medium)
  • ¼ tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 dash ground cloves
  • 1 dash ground ginger

Combine Shakeology, oats, almond butter, and dates in food processor; pulse until dates are incorporated.

Add walnuts, carrots, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger; pulse until just blended.

Roll into fifteen balls, about 2½-cm (1 inch) in diameter each.

Store refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 3 days, or frozen for up to 2 weeks.
